Connect.Share.Heal.

Hang-It-Out is an open platform to help you find or host group hangouts, making it easier to escape loneliness and depression.
Share moments, stories, and support in a truly welcoming space.

Empowering Social Wellbeing

  • Find Real Hangouts: Discover group events focused on authenticity and connection.
  • Host Support Circles: Organize gatherings to share and heal together.
  • Feedback and Growth: Share your journeys and help strengthen the community.
  • Track Progress: Set personal goals and celebrate growth.
  • Privacy & Inclusivity: Connect on your terms in a safe, welcoming space.

How It Works

  1. Explore Events: Browse real hangouts, support meets, and more.
  2. Join or Create: RSVP or host your own gathering—your choice.
  3. Connect & Support: Attend, share, and bond with others.
  4. Progress & Feedback: Reflect and uplift others with your support.
